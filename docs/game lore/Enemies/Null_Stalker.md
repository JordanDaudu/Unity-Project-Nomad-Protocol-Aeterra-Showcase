### 📜 Lore Description
The **Null-Stalker** is the most common manifestation of the Aeterra Blight. These units are composed of high-density nanite clusters that mimic canine-like predatory behavior. They do not possess optical sensors; instead, they track the "thermal heat" signature radiated by a Nomad Unit's processing core. When they gather in packs, they represent the system's primary method of pruning "unauthorized data" from the desert sectors.

## 🛠️ Engineering Breakdown (The "Logic")
The **Null-Stalker** operates on a **Stratified State Machine**, ensuring efficient CPU cycles while maintaining aggressive pursuit behaviors.

* **IDLE:** The unit utilizes a `WanderState`. It patrols random waypoints generated by the `NavMesh` within its assigned "Data Node" to minimize idle processing.
    
* **PURSUIT:** Upon a successful `Sensing_Pulse` detecting the player’s `Transform.position`, the unit transitions to a high-speed chase. Pathfinding is updated dynamically to intercept the player's vector.
    
* **ATTACK:** A proximity-based trigger activates a "Lunge" animation. On the active frame, a `DamageEvent` is broadcast to the player’s health controller.
    
* **RECYCLE (Death):** Upon `CurrentHealth <= 0`, the unit initiates a `CleanupSequence`. It triggers an `ObjectPool.Despawn()` call, dissolving back into purple "nanite dust" to prevent memory fragmentation and ensure high performance during large-scale encounters.
